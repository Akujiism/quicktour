Window management: Stack & Tile

"Stacking" is putting windows on top of each other, automatically moving the yellow tabs into position. While holding the OPT key (normally that's WIN on the keyboard), tabs change color when they overlap; drop the window to establish the stacking.

"Tiling" means gluing windows horizontally or vertically together. While holding OPT, the borders that'll fuse together when you drop the window change color when brought near each other.
